Welcome to TagSieve

Tag Away!

Version 4.3.3

I have been a great fan of the TagSifter extension but unfortunately it hasn't been updated for more than a year now and seems abandoned. I got tired of waiting and decided to work on the extension myself. It took some effort, but I am pleased to announce this release of TagSieve which brings TagSifter to Firefox 3.5 and beyond.

I plan to keep this extension updated and have a few features which I'd like to implement. Meanwhile, feel free to hack on the source, report issues and place your wishlists here. If there is demand for new features, I'll surely look into it.

If you haven't used TagSifter before, to get started have a look at the help.
